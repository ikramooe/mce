Our Company History

2010

Company Founded

Establishment of our company with the vision to provide high-quality metal packaging solutions in the region.

2011

First Production Line Installed

Installation of our first machine for producing easy open ends with 83mm diameter.

2013

Expanded Diameter Range

Added machines to support new diameters: 52mm, 65mm, 73mm, and 99mm for both food and non-food products.

2015

Launched Sardine Can Production

Started the production of two-piece food cans for sardines and similar products.

2017

Varnishing and Cutting Unit Installed

Integration of a cutting and varnishing line to ensure high-finish quality and precision.

2019

Metal Printing Facility

Commissioned our in-house metal printing line, enhancing customization and brand presentation.

2023

Arrival of New Diameters

Expanded our product range with new lid sizes and improvements in can body production.
